Abstract factory

Inent

Provide an interface for creating families of related objects without specifying their concrete class.

Applicability

Cases:
  1. A system should be independent of how its products was created, composed and represented.
  2. A system should be configured with one of multiple families of products.
  3. A family of related product objects is designed to be used together, and you need to enforce this constraint.
  4. You want to provide to reveal only interface for creating objects, not their implementation.

Consequences

Advantages

  1. Abstract Factory encapsulates the responsibilitiy and the process of creating objects and isolates clients from implementation classes.
  2. It makes exchanging objects families easy by changing the concrete factory.
  3. It promotes consinstency among objects families.

Disadvantages

  1. Supporting new kind of objects families is hard. If we'll extends abstract factory we should define all subclasses methods.
  2. Large count of objects families leads to large count abstract factory's methods.
