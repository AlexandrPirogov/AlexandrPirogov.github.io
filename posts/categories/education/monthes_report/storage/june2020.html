The next subject is English. I'm sure that you'll find mistakes in my posts. I've started to practice english: test solving, listening, grammar, reading texts. It takes an hour per day. Right now I'm working with present simple and present continuous. I think the difference between present simple and present continuous is context. When you says about something in present simple you mean that you do it repeatedly. When you says about something in present continuous you mean that you've started do something but not finished.
For example:

  1. "I run every morning" - You run every morning repeatedly. Present Simple
  2. "Hello. I'm running. Can you call me later please?" - When you was running and someone called you and you haven't finished your running.
